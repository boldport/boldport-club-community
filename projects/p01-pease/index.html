--- layout: project project: p01-pease title: "Pease, second edition (Project #1, March 16)" product_page: http://www.boldport.club/shop/product/206712811 osh_files: https://github.com/boldport/pease-2ed hero_image: http://s3.amazonaws.com/cratejoy_vendor_images/813aaf2ca1e743e59512925f36ed5cea.jpeg --- This kit is the very first Boldport Club project. It's a tribute to Bob Pease, a legendary analogue designer, with the circuit based around the LM331, 'voltage to frequency converter' that he designed. All the components for the circuit in Figure 20 of the datasheet: light intensity to frequency converter are provided. Alternatively, surface-mount components can be used (not provided in the kit).
