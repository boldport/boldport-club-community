--- layout: project project: p24-conehead short_name: Conehead title: "Conehead (Project #24, April 18)" shop_page: https://www.boldport.club/shop/product/1432915792 product_page: https://www.boldport.com/products/conehead osh_files: https://github.com/boldport/conehead hero_image: https://static1.squarespace.com/static/539604e8e4b0d1f9ffe9ff0b/t/5ad766711ae6cf109bbc9378/1524065912308/project-24-h1.jpg?format=2500w --- The Conehead is the next series of critter based PCBs (past ones are The Lady, The Gent, and The Monarch). It's an audio circuit that synthesises the sound of a cricket. It uses three oscillators interacting with each other to create the distinctive sound. Using a small speaker and a phototransistor, the cricket will change its chirping frequency depending on the amount of light it 'sees'.
